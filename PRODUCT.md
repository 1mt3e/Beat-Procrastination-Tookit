# Product

## Register

product

## Users
University students who struggle with procrastination, seeking visual clarity and structured guidance to focus.

## Product Purpose
To provide an interactive toolkit combining task planning, mood/energy tracking, Pomodoro timing, structured daily challenges, and reflective journaling.

## Brand Personality
Academic but approachable, authoritative, clear, and visually calming.

## Anti-references
Overcrowded layouts, aggressive saturated gradients, flat boring tables, typical "AI slop" widgets.

## Design Principles
- Minimise Cognitive Load: Glassmorphism and negative space structure information clearly.
- Active Feedback: Instant transitions, crisp visual changes upon input, and helpful toast alerts.
- Structured Focus: Single-panel layout, sequential unlock for the challenge, keeping current tasks highlighted.
